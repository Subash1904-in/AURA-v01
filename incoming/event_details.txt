KSSEM | Department of AI & DS — Final Year Project Exhibition 2025

We are pleased to announce that the Department of Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI & DS), KSSEM is hosting its Final Year Project Exhibition – 2025 on 22 November 2025 at 9:00 AM.

This flagship event celebrates the innovation, technical excellence, and problem-solving abilities of our final-year students as they present projects spanning AI, Machine Learning, Data Science, IoT, Embedded Systems, and Automotive Technology.
